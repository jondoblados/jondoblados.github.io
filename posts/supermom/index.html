Supermom

I love my wife. She’s all about the greater levels of thoughtfulness that I don’t have, and earlier today, she’s outdone herself once more.

In a few hours she had completed all of her Christmas shopping for my god-children and nephews and nieces. No thanks to me; I couldn’t stand gift shopping unless it’s for her or my kids, much worse if it’s for some other people.

Don’t get me wrong, I’m all for giving gifts as long as it’s in the budget, but man, only my wife can think of what stuff to buy. Where was I earlier while my wife was ticking off her shop list? In Roti Mum having coffee and buns! Tsk..

My daughter also got bought some clothes and I can see that she’s very happy with them. Oh well, that’s supermom for you!
